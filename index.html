About Us

Smart Kheti

Smart Kheti is an innovative agricultural project aimed at revolutionizing farming practices through the integration of IoT technology and Web based Module. By deploying IoT sensors in agricultural fields, Smart Kheti collects real-time data on environmental factors like temperature, humidity, and soil moisture. This data is then transmitted to a centralized platform where it can be analyzed Through a user-friendly website linked to the IoT system, farmers gain access to valuable insights and recommendations tailored to their specific location and crop needs. Smart Kheti empowers farmers with the knowledge to optimize resource usage, increase crop yield, and promote sustainable farming practices.
